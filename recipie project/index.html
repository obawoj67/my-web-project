Lasagna Recipe

Back to Odin Recipes

Description

This delicious lasagna is made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and melted cheese. It's a classic comfort food that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a skillet, brown the ground beef with onions and garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce and Italian seasoning to the beef mixture.
  4. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  5. Repeat the layers and top with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
